Experiences

  • Camping

    Camping Safaris...

    Explore Tanzania in a unique way, perfect for nature lovers; this will involve all your senses as you find yourself in the middle of the wilderness. We have affordable packages to cater for everyone’s needs and budget. Imagine surrounding a campfire in the middle of nowhere in the plains of Serengeti, looking at the bright stars shining in the sky as you await your freshly prepared food by professional chefs. If you can’t wait to experience this contact us immediately and we will tailor make it for you.

  • Hiking

    Experience hiking...

    The Arusha Region of northern Tanzania lies on the Kenyan border, encompassing savannahs and part of the Great Rift Valley. It's a notable safari destination and a center of Maasai culture. Nature and wildlife preserves include the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, which contains the massive Ngorongoro Crater, and Arusha National Park, which covers volcanic Mount Meru. Safaris often start in Arusha, the capital city

  • Mountain Climbing

    Experience mountain climbing...

    Climb the African continent’s highest peak and world’s free standing-mountain at 5,895 meters above sea level, the Mount Kilimanjaro. You will have a choice of route that fits your expectations and our experienced guides will assist in building up the package to ensure your climbing experience meets nothing but the best experience of your life. Climbing the mountain will take you through tropical rainforests, savanna landscape, desert landscape, thick ice walls and plains with snow.
    Mt Meru is located in Arusha reaching 4,565 meters high. This trek is considered as an ideal warm up for Mt. Kilimanjaro due to its altitude acclimatization and hiking distance. Contact us for detailed itineraries, routes and prices for this adventure.

  • Wildlife Safari

    Experince wildlife Safari...

    Discover the wilderness by doing game drives, the most important part of wildlife safari. Enjoy Early Morning, Morning, Late Morning, Afternoon, and All Day Game Drive in the national parks. Meet and greet The Big Five and enjoy the spectacular landscape of Tanzania. We will take you to Serengeti National Park (Award winning Africas Leading National Park), Ngorongoro Crater where you will spot the black rhino and the African Big Five, Tarangire National Park where you are sure to see the largest elephant population, Lake Manyara National |Park famous for the eye catching pink flamingos and tree climbing lions, Gombe and Mahale National Park to encounter the chimpanzees in their natural habitats.

  • Culture Tour

    Experience cultural tours...

    Get a chance to engage in cultural activities and learn more about the local peoples culture specifically the lifestyle, the history of the people, their art, architecture, religion and all other elements that have shaped their ways of life. We offer day trips to cultural centers and family home stays. Whatever experience you may wish to have, we will have it catered for you professionally at affordable rates.

  • Beach Holiday

    Experience beach safaris...

    There is something special about Sand, Sea and Water. That is why the Tanzanian Coast boasts itself with exotic sandy beaches and clear blue water. Imagine relaxing in one of your dream holiday beach sipping a freshly squeezed tropical fruit juice. Nothing beats that feeling and that is why we are here to make this dream come true. With our carefully prepared packages, we will guarantee you enjoy the time of your life at the beach. We also offer tailor made packages to suit your needs.
